About Papa

Papa has spent his entire life caring for animals. He believes every pet deserves to look and feel beautiful, no matter their age or breed.

Whether your furry friend needs a relaxing bath, nail trim, paw cleaning, or the full spa treatment, Papa takes his time to make sure every pet feels comfortable, safe, and loved.

One thing everyone notices about Papa is that he loves to tell a good story—and he enjoys hearing one just as much. If you stop by for an appointment, don't be surprised if you leave with a freshly groomed pet and a smile after sharing a story or two.
